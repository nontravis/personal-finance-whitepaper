`NotoSerifCJKsc-*.otf` and `NotoSerifCJKjp-*.otf` are **subsets** of the originals,
reduced to the glyphs used by the Chinese and Japanese editions. The SIL OFL permits
modification and redistribution; the Reserved Font Name "Noto" is retained and these
